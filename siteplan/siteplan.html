Axiom Spaceworks - Site Plan

Site Name

Axiom Spaceworks

This name reflects a futuristic aerospace organization focused on advanced space operations, exploration, and innovation.

Site Purpose

The website will showcase Axiom Spaceworks as a space exploration and engineering organization. It will provide information about spacecraft, missions, services, and how users can join or engage with the organization.

Scenarios

  • What spacecraft does Axiom Spaceworks operate?
  • How can I join or participate in Axiom Spaceworks activities?

Color Schema

#0b0f1a – Background (dark space theme)

#00d4ff – Headings and accents (futuristic glow)

Typography

Orbitron – Headings (futuristic style)

Roboto – Body text (clean and readable)
